    Welcome to the Playground!

    You are at the “Home” screen of this website. This is where you will find out what this website is about.

    To learn about the people who created this fun website, click “About” at the top of the screen. In order of mention, the creators’ names are Aidan Wu, Arch Huang, David Xie, Tyler Hickman, and William Wu.

    And finally, to find what games you can play, click “Games” at the top of the screen. There is a variety of games you can play, from Tic Tac Toe to Knockout. The games can be singleplayer or multiplayer. To navigate between the two, click “Singleplayer” or “Multiplayer” at the bottom of the “Games” screen.

    Create an account by clicking “Sign Up” at the top of the screen. Doing so will save all your game progress as well as high scores. Once you create an account, click “Log In” to log into your account and save all your progress.
